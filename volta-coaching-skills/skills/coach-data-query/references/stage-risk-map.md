# Stage-Risk Map

Quick reference: which risk to focus on at each milestone stage.

| Stage | Revenue | Primary Risk | Coaching Focus |
|-------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Value Baseline | Pre-revenue | 90% Value Risk | Problem exists? Customers will pay? Discovery conversations. |
| First Paying Customer | First $ | Value → Viability | Economics work? Pricing right? Repeatable? |
| X ($10K ARR) | $10K recurring | Feasibility + Viability | Team can scale? Unit economics at volume? |
| 10X ($100K ARR) | $100K @ 20% MoM | Viability + Usability | Venture-scale? Users adopting at scale? |
| $1M ARR | $1M sustained | All low | Institutional risks: governance, hiring, fundraising |

## Key Questions by Risk

**Value Risk:** "Have customers shown willingness to pay?" / "How many discovery conversations outside your network?"
**Feasibility Risk:** "Can the team ship this?" / "What's the shipping cadence?"
**Usability Risk:** "Are users actually using it?" / "What's the onboarding completion rate?"
**Viability Risk:** "Do the unit economics work?" / "What's your CAC:LTV ratio?"
