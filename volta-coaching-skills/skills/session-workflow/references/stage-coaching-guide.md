# Stage-Specific Coaching Guide

Quick reference for what to focus on at each milestone stage.

## Value Baseline (Pre-revenue)

**Objective:** Validate the problem exists and someone will pay.
**Primary Risk:** 90% Value Risk
**Focus:** Customer discovery, problem statement refinement, anti-bias methodology
**Key Questions:**
- "Tell me the problem from the customer's perspective — not the solution"
- "How many people outside your network have you spoken to?"
- "What surprised you in those conversations?"
**Experiments:** 10-20 structured interviews, problem affinity scoring, demo commitment question
**Traps:** Solution-first thinking, network-only interviews, building before validating
**Cadence:** Weekly or bi-weekly, 45-60 min, hands-on

## First Paying Customer

**Objective:** Prove someone will exchange money for this.
**Primary Risk:** Value → Viability shift
**Focus:** Pricing, conversion mechanics, early unit economics
**Key Questions:**
- "What did the customer actually pay for?"
- "How did they find you? Could that channel work again?"
- "What's the lifetime value vs churn cost?"
**Experiments:** Value-based pricing, beta with named customers, channel tests
**Traps:** Underpricing, building instead of selling, one-off revenue
**Cadence:** Bi-weekly, 30 min

## X ($10K ARR)

**Objective:** Build a repeatable growth engine.
**Primary Risk:** Feasibility + Viability
**Focus:** Growth mechanics, channel optimization, team structure
**Key Questions:**
- "What's your growth rate? 20%+ MoM?"
- "What's your primary channel and CAC?"
- "Who does sales full-time?"
**Experiments:** Multi-channel with per-channel CAC, landing page tests, referral programs
**Traps:** Too many channels, hiring too early, ignoring churn
**Cadence:** Bi-weekly, shifting strategic

## 10X ($100K ARR)

**Objective:** Prove this is scalable, not lifestyle.
**Primary Risk:** Viability + Usability
**Focus:** Market positioning, hiring, investment readiness
**Key Questions:**
- "Lifestyle or venture?"
- "What's your hiring plan?"
- "Are you investment-ready?"
**Experiments:** Positioning reframes, first non-founder hire, conference pitches
**Traps:** Founder burnout, not delegating, identity crisis
**Cadence:** Ad-hoc, strategic sessions. Consider Foundation Sprint if needed (see "Click" by Knapp & Zeratsky).

## $1M ARR (Graduation)

**Objective:** Institutional readiness.
**Primary Risk:** Governance, team scaling
**Focus:** Board, organizational design, fundraising or profitability path
**Cadence:** Ad-hoc, connector role
