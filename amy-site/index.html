What This Role Exists To Do

Volta has community strategy (Laura) and Volta has execution (you). This role is the operational engine of Volta's builder community — the person who makes every event excellent, every Sprint session run smoothly, and every builder-leader feel supported. This is a doer role, not a planner role.

Core Responsibilities
  1. 01
    Events Management

    You plan and execute every community event and Sprint session. Venue, logistics, invitations, on-the-day operations, and post-event follow-up. The experience of every person who attends a Volta event is on you.

  2. 02
    Builder Activation

    You design and run the touchpoints that turn passive builders into active community participants. Attendance drives, event programming, and community moments that make people want to show up.

  3. 03
    Sprint Operations

    You manage the operational layer of every Sprint cohort session — logistics, attendance tracking, coordination between builders and facilitators, and post-session documentation.

  4. 04
    Builder-Leader Support

    Volta's builder-leaders run the community at the local level. You are their operational support — helping them run their events, access resources, and stay connected to Volta's programmes.

  5. 05
    Community Data Collection

    You track attendance, engagement, and event outcomes across all community activities. This data feeds directly into Volta's community health systems — it's what makes the whole reporting picture work.

The Day-to-Day

Most of your time is spent on events, activations, and builder relationships — the on-the-ground work that makes the community real. There's an operational and admin layer too: processing, coordination, and follow-through that comes with running regular programming.

What We're Looking For
  • An operator — you follow through on everything, every time
  • An energetic presence — builders feel your energy and it draws them in
  • Detail-oriented — nothing falls through the cracks
  • Relationship-driven — you build genuine connections with the builders you support
  • Adaptable — community work is unpredictable, and you handle it with ease
Performance Indicators
Leading · Q2
  • Event attendance: active builders at ≥1 event/month
  • Sprint session logistics: zero operational failures
  • Builder activation rate: passive → active conversion
  • Data collection: complete for every event
Lagging · Assessed July 1
  • Builder retention: % still engaged 3 months after first event
  • Sprint cohort satisfaction: builder-reported scores
  • Event reach: total unique builders engaged per quarter
