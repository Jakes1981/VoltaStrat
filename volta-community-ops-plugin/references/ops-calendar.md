# Monthly Operations Calendar

## Daily (every working day)
- Application review (30 min) — check new apps, send invites or rejections
- Email/Slack check — monitor residency@voltaeffect.com
- Data hygiene spot check — quick look at Impact OS for gaps

## Week 1 — Launch & Kick-offs
- Coach kick-off calls for new teams (intro email day 1, call within 24hrs)
- Verify all engagements active from previous month's decisions
- No gaps — every seat has an active engagement and assigned coach

## Week 2 — Interviews & Connect
- Conduct all interviews for the month
- Post-interview accept/reject emails
- **Onboarding & Founder Connect Day** (same day: onboard AM, all residents lunch + connect)
- Accepted teams added to eligibility list

## Week 3 — Outreach & Updates
- Email eligible teams for progress updates and interest
- Collect company updates from current residents
- Eligible team response deadline: end of week 3

## Week 4 — Evaluate & Close
- Review expiring seats with coaches
- Risk assessment (Value/Feasibility/Usability/Viability, each /10)
- Decision meeting (Laura + Matt)
- Rejection emails (personalized with feedback)
- Acceptance emails + engagement creation in Impact OS
- Coach assignment + intro emails
- Pre-schedule week 1 kick-offs for next month
- **All complete by last day of the month**
