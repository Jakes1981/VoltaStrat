## Terminology

- **Builders** — people developing AI capability (not users/participants)
- **Vetted** — builder assessed against domain-specific rubrics
- **Impact OS** — the Supabase platform tracking portfolio data
- **Sprint** — cohort-based builder development program
- **Pulse** — community health feedback system
- **Foundation Sprint** — optional structured strategic workshop for teams needing a reset (see "Click" by Knapp & Zeratsky)
- **Problem-first** — start with business problem, never with technology
