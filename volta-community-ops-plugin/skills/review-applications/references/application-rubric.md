# Volta Application Review Rubric (v1.1)

Volta Application → Network → Residency: Unified Grading Rubric

**Purpose:** A single, consistent "+1 / 0 / -1" rubric used from application through ongoing support so evaluations line up end-to-end. Scores guide focus and shortlisting; they **do not** replace judgment. Capture evidence for each score.

## Legend (applies to every criterion)

- **+1 (Yes / Above bar):** Clear, recent evidence; self-directed cadence; outcomes linked to goals.
- **0 (Partial / Needs prodding):** Inconsistent or below expectations; effort without reliable outcomes.
- **-1 (No / Below bar):** Not demonstrated; contradictory to bar; or disallowed.
- **Confidence:** Low / Medium / High (note data quality, e.g., metrics, links, transcripts).

---

## A) Stage 1 Screening — Minimum Criteria (Gate)

**Gate rule:** Any -1 on Location, AI-First Software, or Disallowed Category → **No-Go** (until clarified). Sum is informative only.

### Location — Atlantic Canada

- **+1:** ≥1 full-time founder based in Atlantic Canada; PR/work-permit verified if not a citizen.
- **0:** Some presence or intent but not full-time; status unclear.
- **-1:** No founder based in region; incompatible immigration status.

### AI-First, Software-Only

- **+1:** AI is core to value **or** materially accelerates build/iteration; stack described; software-only.
- **0:** Mentions AI, but peripheral or unproven; unclear stack.
- **-1:** Agency/service; hardware or hardware+software; regulated industry.

### Problem ↔ Solution Fit (software-only)

- **+1:** Specific user/ICP and pain articulated; solution maps directly; early signal (users, LOIs, discovery notes).
- **0:** Problem stated, but ICP/pain light; weak mapping to solution.
- **-1:** Vague problem; solution-first buzzwords; compliance-only drivers.

### Disallowed Category Check

- **+1:** Clearly outside disallowed categories.
- **0:** Mixed signals or pending pivot.
- **-1:** Agency/service, hardware, or regulated (fin/med/ins/legal/cannabis/gambling/aviation, etc.).

**Important distinction:** A product that SERVES customers in regulated industries is NOT itself regulated (e.g., an AI questionnaire tool sold to insurance companies is NOT a regulated product).

### Stage 1 Outputs

- Narrative rationale; risks; Go/No-Go
- If **Go** → schedule Founder & Mindset Fit interview
- If **No-Go** → send rejection + referral

### Rejection Variants

| Variant | Trigger | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| 3D-1 | Insufficient information | Incomplete / Low Context — can't evaluate, need more info |
| 3D-2 | ai_first = -1 | Not AI-First Software |
| 3D-3 | disallowed = -1 | Hardware or Regulated Industry Product |
| 3D-4 | location = -1 | Outside Atlantic Canada |

## B) Founder & Mindset Fit — Interview (V4.0)

Score each of 6 sections using +1 / 0 / -1 and note evidence. Total interview: ~40 minutes.

### 1. Obsession with the Problem (7-8 min)

**Prompts:** Why this problem? If product vanished, what pain would you keep chasing? How do you know customers care?

- **+1:** Lived connection; repeated customer discovery; clear evidence of real pain.
- **0:** Problem stated, weak validation; proxy anecdotes; curiosity not commitment.
- **-1:** Product-only fixation; shallow "opportunity"; no customer signal.

### 2. Differentiation & Founder-Market Fit (7-8 min)

**Prompts:** Why you/this team? What do you know others don't? Current alternatives & why your way wins? If a well-funded rival appears, why do you still win?

- **+1:** Insider insight; unfair advantage; crisp competitor map & wedge.
- **0:** Some edge but shallow; incomplete market map.
- **-1:** Generic "work harder"; no competitor awareness; no edge.

### 3. Grit, Resilience & Operating System (7-8 min)

**Prompts:** Hardest push-through? Time you didn't quit — why? How do you reset?

- **+1:** Specific setbacks; ownership; reliable reset system.
- **0:** Some adversity; ad-hoc resets.
- **-1:** Polished heroics; blame-shifting; no recovery habits.

### 4. Coachability & Speed of Learning (7-8 min)

**Prompts:** Changed your mind recently? Feedback that stung — what changed? After failure, how choose next?

- **+1:** Humility; rapid iteration; structured learning cadence.
- **0:** Accepts feedback but slow to adapt; sporadic experiments.
- **-1:** Defensive; no examples; gut-only decisions.

### 5. Vision, Drive & Endgame (5-6 min)

**Prompts:** 5-year success? If only 20% realized, what's still worth it? Non-negotiable tradeoffs?

- **+1:** Impact-oriented; clear purpose; explicit tradeoffs.
- **0:** Directional; thin tradeoff thinking.
- **-1:** Purely financial; vague ambition; no boundaries.

### 6. Community & Pay-It-Forward (5-6 min)

**Prompts:** Engage peers/advisors? How will you help the next wave? What makes a strong founder community?

- **+1:** Active contributor; concrete give-back habits; ecosystem mindset.
- **0:** Some engagement; unclear give-back plan.
- **-1:** Transactional; no engagement; extractive posture.

### Interview Aggregate Guide

| Sum | Outcome |
|-----|---------|
| ≤ -2 | Keep in Network or No-Go |
| -1 to +2 | Remain in Network; re-evaluate in 30-60 days |
| ≥ +3 | Strong candidate for Residency (capacity permitting) |

## C) Residency Readiness — Entry from Network

Use the same +1 / 0 / -1 anchors to check for upgrade readiness. Assess based on the last 30-60 days of evidence.

### 1. Consistent, focused effort toward $1M+ ARR

- **+1:** Disciplined cadence tied to $1M path.
- **0:** Effort present but needs prodding; inconsistent.
- **-1:** Sporadic; shifting priorities.

### 2. Effort → Results (revenue growth)

- **+1:** Clear revenue growth trajectory.
- **0:** Outcomes below expectations for model/market.
- **-1:** Motion without outcomes.

### 3. Dissatisfied with current pace (hungry to improve)

- **+1:** Self-directed urgency to accelerate.
- **0:** Only when challenged.
- **-1:** Complacent.

### 4. Additional resources available (e.g., capital)

- **+1:** Flexible runway/resources to scale now.
- **0:** Resources tied to narrow projects.
- **-1:** None; no plan to unlock.

### 5. Fast product iteration

- **+1:** Ship-learn-ship rhythm; fast cycles.
- **0:** Delays/disconnect with priorities.
- **-1:** Long cycles; backlog stasis.

### 6. Founders full-time

- **+1:** All core founders full-time.
- **0:** Some founders FT; others split.
- **-1:** Side-projecting.

### 7. All founders engaged in growth

- **+1:** Visible contributions from all.
- **0:** Uneven effort across founders.
- **-1:** Missing/weak contributions.

### 8. Proactive engagement with coach

- **+1:** Proactive; ≥2 touchpoints/week.
- **0:** Yes, but slower cadence.
- **-1:** Reactive or absent.

### 9. Revenue objective in play

- **+1:** Working explicitly toward $1M ARR+.
- **0:** "10X" stretch but sub-$1M path.
- **-1:** "X or lower."

### Residency Upgrade Guide

**Interview ≥ +3** AND **Readiness sum ≥ +3** (last 30-60 days), plus capacity available → accept into Residency. Consider a Foundation Sprint if the team lacks a clear problem hypothesis or 90-day plan.
