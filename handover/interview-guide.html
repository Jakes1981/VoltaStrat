V5.0 — Conversational Framework

02 The Live Coaching Moment
Not a Scheduled Section — Organic but Deliberate
When you spot a weak pitch, a too-broad ICP, a blind spot — step in and coach. This isn't a scheduled section. It happens when the moment is right. The founder's response to live coaching is one of the highest-signal data points in the entire interview.

"What if you framed it this way?"

"Have you considered that X just released Y?"

03 The Five Dimensions
Dimension 1
Problem & Customer Evidence
"Is this founder chasing a real problem with real evidence, or building on assumptions?"
Bundles
Problem clarity Customer discovery rigor ICP specificity Early demand signals
Conversational Starters
  • Tell me about the problem — from your customer's perspective, not yours. Don't tell me about the solution yet."
  • How many people outside your network have you talked to about this?"
  • What surprised you in those conversations?"
  • If your product vanished, what pain would your customers still have?"
Scoring
5Crisp problem from customer's POV. 15+ unbiased conversations. Specific ICP. Revenue/demand signals. Discovery shaped the approach.
4Clear problem, 8–15 real conversations. ICP defined. Some demand signals.
3Problem partly from founder's perspective. 3–7 conversations, mostly network. Broad ICP. No revenue yet.
2Problem vague or shifts when probed. 1–2 conversations. Can't name ICP. Assumptions dominate.
1Solution-first. No customer conversations. Can't describe problem without describing product.
Dimension 2
AI-Native & Technical Depth
"Is AI genuinely core to this product's value, and does this founder understand what that means in today's landscape?"
Bundles
AI centrality Landscape awareness Technical moat beyond AI AI-native tool usage Rebuild test
Conversational Starters
  • What happens if you remove the AI layer — does the product still work?"
  • If someone with the same models started building against you, why do you win?"
  • What's changed in AI in the last 6 months that affects what you're building?"
  • If you started from scratch today, would you build this the same way?"
  • What tools are you building with?"
Scoring
5AI IS the product. Deep landscape understanding. Moat beyond AI. Uses AI-native tools. Has an answer for "AI wrapper" risk.
4AI genuinely central. Understands threats. Defensibility considered. Modern tools.
3AI accelerates but isn't sole value. Aware of trends but hasn't stress-tested commoditization.
2AI is a feature. Could be rules-based. Surface-level understanding. Not using AI tools.
1AI is cosmetic. "We plan to add AI." No technical depth.
Dimension 3
Commercial & Go-to-Market Readiness
"Can this founder sell, not just build?"
Bundles
Pricing clarity GTM strategy ICP narrowing Sales bandwidth Revenue timeline Unit economics
Conversational Starters
  • How do your first 10 paying customers find you?"
  • What will you charge and how did you arrive at that?"
  • Who on the team spends the majority of time on sales?"
  • You mentioned 5 verticals — pick ONE. Which and why?"
  • What does the first $10K revenue look like?"
Scoring
5Paying customers or clear path to revenue in weeks. Pricing tested. Named ICP segment. Dedicated sales bandwidth. Sprint-sized milestones.
4Pricing benchmarked. Beachhead chosen. WTP conversations done. Specific GTM. Revenue timeline defined.
3Pricing considered untested. Category-level ICP. Vague GTM. "6–12 months" timeline.
2Pricing afterthought. Multiple verticals, no focus. No sales effort. "Word of mouth."
1"Build first, sell later." Can't articulate who pays. Engagement metrics as proxy.
Dimension 4
Founder Quality
"Who is this person under pressure, and will they grow through coaching?"
Bundles
Grit / resilience Coachability (live coaching moment) Learning speed Self-awareness Focus Team dynamics
Conversational Starters
  • Tell me about a time this didn't go how you expected."
  • What's the biggest mistake you've made so far?"
  • What are you worst at?"
  • If you had to choose one [venture], which and why?"
  • Multi-founder: "Walk me through the last real disagreement."
Scoring
5Specific adversity with ownership. Absorbs coaching immediately. Names blindspots unprompted. Focused. Healthy team dynamic.
4Real resilience. Responds well to coaching. Knows where they need help. Focused. Good dynamic.
3Polished adversity stories. Accepts coaching slowly. Partial self-awareness. Mostly focused.
2Generic resilience. Needs multiple probes. Limited self-awareness. Split across ventures.
1Blame-shifting. Defends against coaching. No self-awareness. Not focused. Unclear team roles.
Dimension 5
Alignment & Commitment
"Is Volta's model the right fit for where this founder is right now?"
Bundles
Execution bandwidth (20+hrs) Stage fit Program fit Vision-to-milestone connection Community orientation
Founders don't need to be full-time. The standard is 20+ hours/week on sales AND 20+ hours/week on product across the founding team.
Conversational Starters
  • What does success look like in 12 months? Next 90 days?"
  • What kind of support is most valuable to you right now?"
  • What would you sacrifice to make this work?"
  • How are sales hours and product hours covered across your team?"
Scoring
520+20 hrs covered with clear ownership. Perfect stage fit. Vision connects to 90-day milestones. Wants execution coaching specifically. Community contributor.
4Hours close. Roles defined. Good fit. Excited about accountability. Community-positive.
3Hours sufficient but imbalanced (heavy product, light sales). Acceptable stage. Surface understanding of Volta. Directional vision.
2Hours marginal. Sales an afterthought. Stage may be wrong. Wants funding/space, not coaching. Generic vision.
1Can't commit hours. No sales planned. Wrong stage. Wants a badge. Extractive posture.

06 Principles
1
Listen more than you talk. Founder speaks 70%+.
2
Specific over impressive. Raw beats rehearsed.
3
"Don't tell me the solution" is your sharpest tool.
4
Coach in the conversation — the response IS the interview.
5
One positive response doesn't mean coachable. Count your probes.
6
Absence of evidence is evidence.
7
Score immediately. Write evidence, not just numbers.
8
This is mutual fit. No-Go isn't a judgment on the founder.

This framework was designed from analysis of 18 actual Volta founder interviews. It captures what produced the strongest signal and drops what didn't. Trust the conversation. Coach when you see the moment. Score while it's fresh.
