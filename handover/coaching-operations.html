02

Session Workflow

Before the Session
  • Coach reviews company context (latest updates, metrics, previous session notes)
  • Previously: calendar prep agent generated 3-bullet prep cards automatically
  • Going forward: Laura ensures company context is current in Impact OS; coaches pull their own prep from the system
  • Key prep data: company stage, last session commitments, recent metrics, known risks
During the Session
  • notetaker@voltaeffect.com is added to the calendar invite for Fireflies transcription
  • Fireflies records, transcribes, and generates AI summary
  • Coach conducts session focusing on: progress against commitments, current blockers, next milestones
  • Minimum: clear commitments must be set for both the founder and the coach
After the Session
  • Fireflies transcript lands in the system (synced to Supabase meeting_transcripts table)
  • Action items should be tracked — who committed to what, by when
  • Company observations updated in Impact OS
  • Follow-up communication sent to founder (recap + commitments)
03

Fireflies Transcription Setup

  • Volta's Fireflies account is connected to notetaker@voltaeffect.com
  • To record a meeting: add notetaker@voltaeffect.com as an attendee on the Google Calendar invite
  • Fireflies will join the meeting automatically and begin recording
  • The AI notetaker is visible to all participants
  • Transcripts are stored in Supabase (meeting_transcripts table) and matched to company interactions
  • The Fireflies API key is configured in the system — if it stops working, the token may need to be refreshed from the Fireflies dashboard under the Volta account
Privacy Note: If a founder says “remove the note taker” or asks the bot to leave, everything after that point is off-record. Do not reference off-record content in any documentation or follow-up.

06

Commitment Tracking

  • Every session should produce clear commitments from both sides
  • Coach commitments: introductions, resource sharing, follow-up actions
  • Founder commitments: milestones to hit, experiments to run, data to collect
  • Previously tracked in action_items table in Supabase with owner attribution (volta vs. founder)
  • Action items have: text, owner_type, meeting_date, status (open/resolved), created_at, resolved_at
  • Overdue threshold: 14+ days without resolution
Minimum standard: No session should end without at least one clear commitment from the founder.

Coaching is Volta's most direct mechanism for creating founder success. The operations around it — scheduling, recording, tracking, following up — are what turn good conversations into real progress. Keep the system running and the loops closed.
