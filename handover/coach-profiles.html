Coach Matching Guide

A practical reference for assigning coaches to founders. Consider these factors when making a match:

  1. Domain expertise alignment — match coach background to founder's industry/challenge
  2. Stage alignment — some coaches are better at early stage (validation), others at growth (scaling)
  3. Personality fit — contrarian thinkers pair differently than steady guides
  4. Geographic proximity — for in-person coaching (Shri for Cape Breton, others for Halifax, Jaco remote only)
  5. Capacity — check current portfolio load before assigning
  6. Existing relationships — continuity matters; avoid switching coaches mid-engagement unless necessary
