What This Role Exists To Do

This role is the relational and architectural layer of Volta's builder community. Strategy — the OKRs, the direction, what Volta is building — belongs to Jaco and Matt. This role is the implementation of the community experience, relationships, and data infrastructure that makes the ecosystem function.

Volta has 300+ builders, an active coaching bench, and a Sprint programme that needs community signal to run well. None of that works without someone dedicated to making the human layer excellent. That's this role.

Core Responsibilities
  1. 1 · Community Experience Design
    You design and maintain the experience of being part of Volta's builder community — the rhythm, communication, events architecture, and how 300+ people feel about Volta. This is architectural work, not operational logistics; the logistics layer sits with Amy.
  2. 2 · Builder-Leader Programme
    You own relationships with Volta's builder-leaders — the 20–30 leaders who make the community self-sustaining beyond any one person. These relationships are the long-term foundation of the whole community layer.
  3. 3 · Coach Coordination
    You manage the contracted coaching bench for the Startup Residency — roster, briefings, scheduling, and 1:1 check-ins. Coaches report to you; Jaco leads their ongoing development and training.
  4. 4 · Community Data Quality
    You own the accuracy and currency of Impact OS and Pulse. Jaco and Matt read directly from the system — your deliverable is clean, current data. Data quality is not a support function here; it's a core accountability.
  5. 5 · Sprint Community Synthesis
    You track builder demand and experience, surface patterns to Matt for Sprint planning. You don't own Sprint design — Matt does. You inform it with the ground-level community lens no one else holds.
The Day-to-Day
70–75% of your time

Relationships, synthesis, and experience architecture — the relational and intellectual work that is the core of this role.

25–30% of your time

Operational tasks — applications, onboarding, coach invoicing. Volta's AI stack handles the heavy lifting, but these tasks are yours to own.

What We're Looking For
  • A relationship builder — you've built genuine connections at scale and know what makes people feel part of something
  • A structured thinker — you can hold complexity without losing the human element
  • A data owner — good decisions come from clean data; you own the systems
  • An operator — you follow through; nothing drops
