04

Questions for Matt

Three things we need your input on before we proceed
Jaco's proposal is ready to move — these are the gates
1
Does "Director of Community & Programs" land well externally?
With funders (ACOA, Province), with builders, and with the team. "Product Director" was considered but felt too tech-startup. This title leads with community and adds the program scope. Does it work for how you'd introduce Laura to stakeholders?
2
Any concerns about the COO transition timing relative to Laura's onboarding?
Laura's first 30 days are ops-heavy — data audit, coach briefing rhythms, portfolio mapping. Jaco's title change is happening in parallel. Is the timing clean, or does one need to land before the other publicly?
3
Are you comfortable with Amy's title change happening at the same time as Laura's hire announcement?
The intent is to frame both changes together — "we're building out the community and programs function properly, and here's how the two roles sit." Done well, Amy feels recognised. Done poorly, it reads as a demotion. Do you want to be in the room when Jaco has that conversation with Amy?
